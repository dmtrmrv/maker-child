== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the 'Add New' button.
2. Click 'Upload Theme' in the top part of the screen. 
3. Click 'Choose File' locate maker-child.zip on your computer and click 'Install Now'.
4. After installation is complete, click 'Activate'.
